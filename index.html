AeroPlaneGame

AeroPlaneGame is simple game created using JAVA Applet.

When applet starts aero plane moves and score increases. You have to avoid obstacles and finally if you score above 500 then you will win otherwise you lose.

AeroPlaneGameInfo.pdf gives complete information of AeroPlaneGame with snapshots of game.
